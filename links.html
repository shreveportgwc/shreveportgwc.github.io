    Important Links

    Below are the different websites we will be using during the year.

    Canvas

    Canvas

    • Contains all the curriculum and resources that you will use during the club.
    • Keeps track of the projects you will work on under the "Modules" tab.
    • Allows for submission of code and URLs for each assignment, as well as automatically grade tests. This is how you will turn in assignments.
    • Allows for club discussions on important topics. Start discussions when you have questions!

    Codesters: Level 1

    Codesters

    • Level 1 students will do all of their assingments on Codesters.
    • You will learn the foundations of programming by learning a language called Python.
    • In the beginning, you can drag and drop pieces of code already made for you. As you gain confidence, you can switch to the text view to use text code.

    Khan Academy: Level 2

    Khan Academy

    • Level 2 students will begin the year using Khan Academy. You will be coding in Javascript.
    • You will add to your knowledge you gained during Level 1. You do not have drag and drop blocks, but the assignments have plently of instructions to get started.
    • As you advance, you will eventually work indepently, but Khan Academy is a great resource for looking for help and guidance.
